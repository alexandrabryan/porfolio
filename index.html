About Me

As a former-teacher-turned-developer, I'm inspired to create build software that makes a difference.

Extensive experience in conceptualizing, planning, and creating products means I always have the end user in mind - from email marketing campaigns and web applications, to blog posts and goal-based curriculum. I also love to learn, and share that learning with others.

I graduated from the Moms Can Code School in 2019, a coding and professional development program specifically for moms transitioning into tech roles. I'm also a certified secondary English teacher, holding a B.A. in Journalism and Communications from Rowan University and a M.A. in Teaching from California University of Pennsylvania.
